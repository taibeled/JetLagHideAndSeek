# ODPT Data Notice

This directory contains processed public transportation data derived from GTFS
files published through the Public Transportation Open Data Center / Association
for Open Data of Public Transportation.

## Attribution

When using, displaying, or redistributing the processed data in this directory,
include attribution to:

- Public Transportation Open Data Center / Association for Open Data of Public
  Transportation
- Tokyo Metro Co., Ltd. for Tokyo Metro GTFS-derived data
- Tokyo Metropolitan Bureau of Transportation for Toei Subway GTFS-derived data

Suggested attribution text:

> Contains public transportation data made available by the Public
> Transportation Open Data Center / Association for Open Data of Public
> Transportation, including data provided by Tokyo Metro Co., Ltd. and the Tokyo
> Metropolitan Bureau of Transportation. The data has been processed for use in
> Jet Lag Hide and Seek.

Japanese:

> 本アプリケーションには、公共交通オープンデータセンター／公共交通オープンデータ協議会が公開するデータ（東京地下鉄株式会社および東京都交通局提供データを含む）を加工したデータが含まれます。
