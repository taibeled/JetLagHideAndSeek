# ODPT Sources

See [NOTICE.md](./NOTICE.md) for attribution, license, and usage-rule notes
that apply to the processed ODPT data checked into this directory.

## Toei

- Provider: Tokyo Metropolitan Bureau of Transportation
- GTFS file: https://api-public.odpt.org/api/v4/files/Toei/data/Toei-Train-GTFS.zip
- Route: https://api-public.odpt.org/api/v4/odpt:Railway?odpt:operator=odpt.Operator:Toei
- Station: https://api-public.odpt.org/api/v4/odpt:Station?odpt:operator=odpt.Operator:Toei

## Tokyo Metro

- Provider: Tokyo Metro Co., Ltd.
- GTFS file: https://api.odpt.org/api/v4/files/TokyoMetro/data/TokyoMetro-Train-GTFS.zip?acl:consumerKey=[YOUR_ACCESS_TOKEN]
- Route: https://api.odpt.org/api/v4/odpt:Railway?odpt:operator=odpt.Operator:TokyoMetro&acl:consumerKey=[YOUR_ACCESS_TOKEN]
- Station: https://api.odpt.org/api/v4/odpt:Station?odpt:operator=odpt.Operator:TokyoMetro&acl:consumerKey=[YOUR_ACCESS_TOKEN]
